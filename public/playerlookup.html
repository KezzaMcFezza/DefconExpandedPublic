However not all infractions are equally bad so i have included some extra information such as the raw information from the database so you can be the judge if the infraction upon that player is valid. The algorithm that DefconExpanded uses is purely a match based system. So its safe to assume that if an infraction has occured then its most likely legit.

I also included a very simple rating system, mainly because i was bored and everyone loves numbers.
Every player starts at a rating of 50, this means highest trust/rating which indicates they have no infractions on their record. Every infraction removes 7 points off your record which is shown in colors since everyone loves colors, and yellow basically means who cares probably just happened once. Orange means that a player in question has done this more than once and means they might regularly smurf. And red basically means full on drum and bass smurf.
