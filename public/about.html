Welcome to DefconExpanded

Welcome to the Official Website for DefconExpanded, where you can view recordings, download Defcon resources and track player statistics. We have implemented numerous quality of life improvements for the Vanilla and modding playerbase, including Discord integration, advanced game tracking, and comprehensive statistical analysis. Our modded Defcon and Dedcon clients support 8-16 players and have been ported to MacOS and Linux, making the game more accessible than ever. With seasonal tournaments, advanced filtering systems, and a rich modding community, we're dedicated to enhancing the DEFCON experience.
